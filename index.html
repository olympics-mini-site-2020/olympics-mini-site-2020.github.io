ABOUT HAIKU POEM

Ahead of the Tokyo 2020 Olympics and Paralympics, we invite you to call on the Japanese government to introduce a law that protects LGBTI people from discrimination.

Japan, as the host country of the games, has not taken sufficient measures to protect lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and intersex (LGBTI) people who face pervasive discrimination in their daily lives.

We believe that no one should live and love under the fear of discrimination. Join us in uniting our world in diversity.
